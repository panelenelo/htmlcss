Experiences

  • 1889-1890: Studied at the Royal Danish Academy of Fine Arts
  • 1891-1900: Married to Peder Severin Krøyer, a prominent Danish painter
  • 1902-1905: Lived in Paris and studied under the French painter, Pierre Puvis de Chavannes
  • 1905-1912: Married to Swedish painter, Hugo Alfvén
  • 1912-1920: Lived in Sweden and continued to paint
  • 1920-1940: Returned to Denmark and lived in Skagen, where she continued to paint until her death in 1940
